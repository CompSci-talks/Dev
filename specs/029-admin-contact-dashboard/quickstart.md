# Quickstart: Admin Contact Moderation

## Setup
1. Ensure you have administrator privileges (your UID is in the `admins` collection or handled by `AdminGuard`).
2. Navigate to `/admin/feedback` via the browser or the sidebar link.

## Common Tasks

### Reviewing Messages
- The list shows the 50 most recent messages.
- Click any row to open the **Slide-over Panel**.
- The panel contains the full message text and the sender's email.

### Updating Status
- Use the status toggle/dropdown in the list or detail view.
- Marking as **'resolved'** indicates the thread is closed.

### Replying to Users
- Click the **'Reply'** button in the list or detail view.
- This will redirect you to the platform's internal **Email Composer** with the recipient and subject context pre-filled.

### Deleting Messages
- Click the **'Delete'** icon to soft-delete a message.
- Once deleted, it will no longer appear in the moderation dashboard.
